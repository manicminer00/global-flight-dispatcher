📍 Add New Airport (to Local DB)

Why do I need to choose between Turboprop and Business Jet?
📍 Even if a runway is physically long enough for Business Jets, it might not allow them due to noise restrictions, permits, weight-bearing limits on the tarmac, or a lack of specific FBO facilities. This option allows you to designate a 3000-6000ft runway as "Turboprop only" or "Business Jet capable."

💾 Custom Database Backup

N.B. Any custom aircraft and airports that you have added to your local database will be removed if you empty your browser cache. Use the buttons below to create and restore a local backup of your custom database before emptying your browser cache. Logbook Entries are included in the exported file.
Reset Custom Database
What does Reset Custom Database do?
Step-by-Step Process:
  • 1. Confirmation: A dialog asks for permission to wipe custom data.
  • 2. Deletion: Only the (dispatcher_custom_airports, and dispatcher_custom_fleet) keys are removed from your browser's local memory.
  • 3. Reassurance: This does not clear cookies, bookmarks, or browsing history; it only affects this specific dispatcher tool.
  • 4. What about my Logbook?: 'Reset Custom Database' only deletes custom airframes and custom airports that you have manually added. It does not clear your Logbook or remove your Owned Airport list.
  • 5. Clean up: The interface instantly updates to reflect the empty database.
